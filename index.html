What Every Character Needs

This tool is built to reduce the problem we ran into with Ava: inconsistent tattoos, wrong angles, flipped feet, drifting body features, and unreliable back shots. Better references create better characters.

1
6 Headshots

Front, slight left, slight right, smile, serious, natural expression.

2
2 Full Body Photos

One front view and one rear or side view whenever possible.

3
Character Bible

Name, age, birthplace, personality, backstory, style, audience, and goals.

4
Tattoo / Body Map

Exact side, exact placement, what is allowed, and what is forbidden.

Output Goal
